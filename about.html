Swachata Abhiyan (Cleanliness Drive):Seva for Serenity

Tapi Mata Aarti is a sacred spiritual initiative conducted on the banks of the holy Tapi River. Rooted in devotion, tradition, and community service, the Aarti brings together devotees every week to experience divine serenity through light, music, and prayer.

Organized by dedicated sevaks and volunteers, the initiative also focuses on cleanliness drives, cultural awareness, and social upliftment‚Äîensuring that spirituality and responsibility go hand in hand.

Aarti Training: Learn the Art of Devotion

Step into the divine rhythm of devotion! Our monthly aarti training sessions welcome new members to learn the sacred practices of the aarti.

From rituals to etiquettes, these sessions ensure the tradition thrives, passing on its discipline and devotion to every new sevak.
